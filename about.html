About Me

Hi there! My name is Garrett Gilbert and I am an aspiring Voice Actor from the East Coast!

I grew up in the northern panhandle of West Virginia. I got my Bachelor's Degree in Communication Studies with a minor in Business Administration at West Virginia University. I have a very strong passion for Voice Acting and have been working my hardest to get my career started. I am dedicated, hard working and have quick turn around on projects I am given. If you or someone you know needs voice over work done right, please feel free to contact me via Facebook, Twitter, or email. My Facebook and Twitter links are at the bottom of the page and you can reach my email by clicking the Contact tab above!
