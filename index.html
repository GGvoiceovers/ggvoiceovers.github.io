My Story

Hi there! My name is Garrett Gilbert and I am an aspiring Voice Actor.

I grew up in the northern panhandle of West Virginia. I got my Bachelor's Degree in Communication Studies with a minor in Business Administration at West Virginia University. I have a very strong passion for Voice Acting and have been working my hardest to get my career started. I am dedicated, hard working and have quick turn around on projects I am given. If you or someone you know needs voice over work done right, please feel free to contact me via Facebook, Twitter, or email.
